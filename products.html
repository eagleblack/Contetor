CONTETOR EVENTS.

Events are a competitive approch towards digital content creation.

Events are limited entry E-competition, either FREE/PAID between creator of same content category.

    Basic Facts about events.

  • Only Influencer can create Events.
  • Events should be always limited entry not exceeding maximum participent of 40/event.This has been done keeping in mind that every creator should get equal chance of winning.
  • Events can be both free and paid.
  • In case of paid event Influencer will have to redistribute back the entry fee back to winner choosing one of our algoritham.
    Influencer can't keep Entry fee amount to themselves.
  • Events participation and winning highly impact credit score of creator.
  • If Advertisment is shown within Events Contetor shares revenue with "Influencer".
  • Winning Posts from Events hosted by Contetor will be promote before any other post inside the advertisment Model
