Credit Engine.

Check Your creator Credit score based on your Instagram profile.

This credit Engine uses Instagram Graph Api by facebbok to access your Instagram Account.

Info! We never store any data from your Instagram Account except for Username and final credit score.
Your Accounts will be auto logout once your credit score is checked.

Before you start checking your credit score make sure you have following alreday set up.

  • An Instagram acount with minimum of 100 followers.
  • That profile must be active within one month time.
  • Must have 5 post(video and reels not supported currently.)
  • A facebook account and a Facebook page linked with your instagram account.

Once You are ready Click on Continue with facebbok button below to Start checking your credit score.
How Does it work?

  • Once you click on continue with facebook a login dialog will pop-up from facebook.
    Login into your facebook account.
  • If your facebook account has multiple page with it.
    Grab the page id of the page that your instagram account is linked with and enter it in the input box and click on continue.
  • Verify the instagram profile that you want to check credit score for.
  • Sit back and relax while system generate credit score for you.
    It might take 2-5 minute to complete depending on your network.
